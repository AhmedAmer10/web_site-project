BREAKFAST

"Cast-Iron Scrambled Eggs"

Prep/Total Time: 10 min.
Ingredients
    12 large eggs           1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
      

  2 tablespoons water       2 tablespoons butter
      

  1/4 teaspoon salt         1 log (4 ounces) fresh goat cheese, crumbled
      

  1/4 teaspoon pepper       3 tablespoons minced chives
      

  2/3 cup finely chopped sweet onion
      

         
STEPS

  (1)In a large bowl, whisk the eggs, water, salt and pepper; set aside.

  (2)Place a 10-in. cast-iron skillet on grill rack over medium-hot heat.
    In the skillet, saute onion and jalapeno in butter until tender. Add
    egg mixture; cook and stir until almost set. Stir in cheese and chives;
    cook and stir until eggs are completely set.


"Bacon Egg Cups"

Prep/Total Time: 20 min.
Ingredients
  4 bacon strips                       1/8 teaspoon pepper
      

  4 large eggs                        1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
      

  1/3 cup half-and-half cream         2 green onions, chopped
      


         
STEPS

  (1)In a small skillet, cook bacon over medium heat until cooked but not crisp.
     Remove to paper towels to drain; keep warm.

  (2)In a small bowl, whisk 2 eggs, cream and pepper. Wrap 2 bacon strips around
     the inside edges of each of two 8-oz. ramekins or custard cups
      coated with cookingspray.

  (3)Sprinkle each with half of the cheese and onions. Divide egg mixture between
      ramekins. Break 1 of the remaining eggs into each ramekin. Sprinkle with remaining
      cheese and onion. Bake at 350° until eggs are completely set, 18-22 minutes.


LUNCH

"Rosemary Salmon and Veggies"

Prep/Total Time:40 min.
Ingredients
  1-1/2 pounds salmon fillets, cut into 4 portions      1/2 teaspoon salt
      

  2 tablespoons melted coconut oil or olive oil         1 pound fresh asparagus, trimmed
      

  2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar                        1 medium sweet red pepper,
                                                        cut into i-inche pieces

      

  2 teaspoons minced fresh rosemary or                  1/4 teaspoon pepper
  3/4 teaspoon dried rosemary, crushed
      

  1 garlic clove, minced                                Lemon wedges
      

         
STEPS

  (1)Preheat oven to 400°. Place salmon in a greased 15x10x1-in. baking pan.
    Combine oil, vinegar, rosemary, garlic and salt. Pour half over salmon.
    Place asparagus and red pepper in a large bowl; drizzle with remaining
    oil mixture and toss to coat. Arrange around salmon in pan; sprinkle
  (2)Bake until salmon flakes easily with a fork and vegetables are tender,
    12-15 minutes. Serve with lemon wedges.


"Cheddar Jalapeno Chicken Burgers"

Prep/Total Time:30 min.
Ingredients
  1/2 cup finely chopped onion                      3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
      

 1/3 cup finely shredded cheddar cheese            1/4 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
      

  1/4 cup minced fresh cilantro                     1-1/2 pounds ground chicken
                                                    1-1/2 pounds ground chicken
      

  2 garlic cloves, minced                             4 hamburger buns, split
      

  2 teaspoons finely chopped jalapeno pepper          1/2 cup guacamole
      

  2 teaspoons finely chopped jalapeno pepper          1/2 cup guacamole

         
STEPS

  (1)In a large bowl, combine the first nine ingredients. Add chicken; mix
     lightly but thoroughly. Shape into four 3/4-in. thick patties.

  (2)Moisten a paper towel with cooking oil; using long-handled tongs, rub
    on grill rack to coat lightly. Grill burgers, covered, over medium heat
   7-8 minutes on each side or until a thermometer reads 165°. Serve on buns
   with guacamole and additional toppings as desired.

"Spanish Marinated Mushroom"

Prep/Total Time:15 min.
Ingredients
    2 cups water                           2 garlic cloves, minced
      

    1 cup red wine vinegar                 1-1/2 teaspoons salt
      

    3 tablespoons olive oil                1 teaspoon dried oregano
      

    1 tablespoon sugar                     1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
      

    1 tablespoon dried basil               2 pounds small fresh mushrooms

      

         
STEPS

  (1)In a large shallow dish, combine the first 9 ingredients. Add the
     mushrooms and turn to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 8 hours or
     overnight.

  (2)Drain mushrooms, discarding marinade.


"Mini Cheese Balls"

Prep/Total Time:30 min.
Ingredients
  2-1/2 pounds chicken wings                 Oil for deep-fat frying
      

  1/2 cup all-purpose flour                  2 tablespoons butter, melted
      

  2 teaspoons salt                           1-1/2 teaspoons lemon-pepper seasoning
      

  1/4 teaspoon pepper                        2 tablespoons minced fresh parsley
      

         
STEPS

  (1)Cut wings into 3 sections; discard wing tip sections. In a large bowl,
    combine flour, salt and pepper. Add wings, a few at a time, and toss to coat.


  (2)In an electric skillet or deep fryer, heat oil to 375°. Fry wings, a few at a
    time, until no longer pink, 3-4 minutes on each side. Drain on paper towels.
    In a large bowl, combine butter and seasoning. Add wings; toss to coat. Sprinkle
    with parsley. Serve immediately.


APPETIZERS

"Chocolate Peanut Butter No-Bake Cookies"

Prep/Total Time:20 min.
Ingredients
  2 cups sugar                Dash salt

      

  1/2 cup butter or margarine, cubed                1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
      

  1/2 cup 2% milk                                   1 teaspoon vanilla extract
      

  3 tablespoons baking cocoa                        3 cups old-fashioned oats
      

         
STEPS

  (1)In a large saucepan, combine the first 5 ingredients. Bring to a boil,
    stirring constantly. Cook and stir 3 minutes.



  (2)Remove from heat; stir in peanut butter and vanilla until blended. Stir
    in oats. Drop mixture by tablespoonfuls onto waxed paper-lined baking sheets.
    Refrigerate until set. Store in airtight containers.


"Healthy Peanut Butter Cookies"

Prep/Total Time:15 min.
Ingredients
  1 large egg, beaten
      

  1 cup creamy peanut butter
      

  1/4 cup oat bran
      

  1/4 cup maple syrup
      

         
STEPS

  (1)In a large bowl, mix all ingredients. Roll level tablespoons into balls.
     Place on ungreased baking sheets; flatten with a fork.

  (2)Bake at 350° for 15 minutes. Remove to a wire rack to cool.
